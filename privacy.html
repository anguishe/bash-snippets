Cookies

This site itself does not set first-party cookies. Third-party services (Google AdSense, Google Analytics) may set their own cookies. You can control cookie behaviour through your browser settings or by using a browser extension like uBlock Origin.

Some links on this site may be affiliate links. This means if you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. Affiliate links are labelled or noted near the link. We only link to products we would recommend regardless of commission.

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

Since we don't collect personal data directly, there is typically nothing to request deletion of. If you have concerns about data collected by third-party services (Google, GitHub), please refer to their respective privacy policies linked above.

Changes to this policy
