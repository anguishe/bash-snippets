What to Send

Concrete details beat vibes. Paths, logs, reproduction steps, and what you expected vs. what happened all land fixes faster.

Script corrections or bugs you've found Paste the snippet name, flags, distro, bash version, and what broke—repro beats guesswork.
Bash/Linux topics you'd like to see covered Call out workflows you hate doing manually—cron quirks, systemd timers, portability pitfalls, whatever.
Tool ideas or feature suggestions Generators, explainers, validators—anything that trims trial-and-error inside the BashSnippets tooling.
Partnership or collaboration proposals Sponsorships, cross-promos, or technical collabs stay welcome when they respect readers.
